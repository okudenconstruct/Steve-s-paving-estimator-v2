Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Paving Calculator is a construction estimating tool that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and estimates are performed locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Access

The app requires no internet connection to function. All features work fully offline.

Data Storage

Any data you enter into the app (project dimensions, production rates, costs) exists only in your current session and is not persisted to disk or transmitted anywhere.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
